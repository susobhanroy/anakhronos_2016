"ANAKHRONOS", THE ANNUAL CULTURAL FESTIVAL OF TECHNO INDIA, IS HELD IN FEBRUARY EVERY YEAR. IT IS ONE OF THE BIGGEST FEST IN KOLKATA WITH A CUMULATIVE FOOTFALL OF AROUND 20,000 IN A SPAN OF 3 DAYS. ANAKHRONOS WAS STARTED BY A GROUP OF YOUNG ENTHUSIASTS IN THE YEAR 2003, AND IT HAS NOW BECOME A TRADITION. IN PAST, ANAKHRONOS HAS BEEN A STAGE FOR THE MOST UNIQUE AND ENTERTAINING PERFORMANCES IN INDIA. SOME OF THE MOST OUTSTANDING PERFORMANCES OVER THE PAST FEW YEARS ARE THOSE BY "EUPHORIA, STRINGS, PENTAGRAM, KK, SHAAN, KUNALGANJAWALA, FOSSILS, PARIKRAMA, SPUNK, AXE-TORTION, USHAUTTUP, CHANDRABINDOO, ABHIJEET, PARASHPATHOR, CACTUS, ASH KING,MD IRFAN,RAETH AND MANY MANY MORE". ANAKHRONOS IS DERIVED FROM THE GREEK WORD ANACHRONISMS WHICH CAN BE INTERPRETED AS "MOMENTS SURPASSING TIME"... AND YES, WE DO LIVE UP TO ITS MEANING..! IT IS INDEED ONE OF THE MOST PROMISING OPPORTUNITIES FOR STUDENTS TO STAGE THEIR TALENTS. THIS YEAR BEING THE 10TH VERSION, WE PROMISE TO MAKE IT BIGGER... BETTER... LARGER THAN EVER BEFORE!

Rules
1. Team Size: 4-10 members. At least 1 vocalist and 3 different instruments required (different guitars counted individually) and one percussion instrument compulsory.
2. Time Limit: 10+5 minutes from empty stage to empty stage (including soundcheck).
3. Songs can be of any language. Any swearing on stage will lead to disqualification.
4. Equipments provided will be: a. Adequate Microphones b. Drum Kit
5. Bands will have to bring their own musical instruments, brass section and special effects. However, programmed music is not allowed.
6. Marks will be deducted for exceeding time limit.
7. A warning bell will be given at the end of 10 minutes.
8. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
9. Team Anakhronos will not be responsible for failure of any instruments.
10. Team Anakhronos reserves the right to make any last minute changes in rules and has the right to disqualify a participating band on grounds of misconduct.
